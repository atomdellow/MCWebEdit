üéØ How It Works

  • Multi-face blocks like grass have different textures for top, side, and bottom faces
  • Directional blocks like logs have different textures for their ends vs. sides
  • Simple blocks like stone use the same texture on all faces
  • The BlockPalette shows the top texture as a preview with a ‚ö° indicator for multi-face blocks
  • In the 3D editor, each face renders with its correct texture for realistic appearance
